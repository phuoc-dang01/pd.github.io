About

I'm a PhD candidate in Informatics at NJIT, studying privacy in machine learning. I study how representations carry information across distributed AI systems, how to quantify that leakage, and how to defend against it.

My current focus is embedding and hidden-state inversion attacks against split and hybrid inference. Before this, I spent four years applying machine learning to air traffic management.
